Ramen and noodle Course Ramen, a noodle soup dish is one of the most popular Japanese foods around the world. Originated in China, Ramen has evolved distinctively in Japan over time and Ramen restaurants can be found anywhere in Japan and each area has its own unique regional variations.
Kaiseki Course This course teaches students, who already have a basic Japanese cuisine experience, the concept of Kaiseki cuisine and its technique for one week. Kaiseki cuisine refers to the dishes served for entertaining guests in the event of a tea ceremony.
